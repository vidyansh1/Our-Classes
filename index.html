Meet Director of Ourclasses

Rameez Mirza Sir

10+ Years Experience Hundreds Mentored Custom Roadmaps

Rameez Mirza is a visionary Civil Services mentor with 10+ years of experience in coaching and guiding UPSC aspirants. He strongly believes that in today's competitive UPSC environment, 1:1 mentorship is the key differentiator between effort and success.

His mentorship philosophy goes beyond syllabus completion. It focuses on:

  • Personalized strategy building
  • Answer writing and thought process development
  • Consistent performance tracking
  • Mental conditioning and confidence building
  • Real-time course correction

Over the years, Rameez Mirza has mentored hundreds of aspirants, many of whom credit their breakthrough to his hands-on approach, honest feedback, and unwavering support. He doesn't believe in generic plans; he believes in custom roadmaps designed for each aspirant's reality.
